Baked Sardines

Description

Oven-baked sardines with parsley and garlic

Ingredients:

for

  • 200 g sardines
  • to coat sardines - high quality oil for high temperatures (ex.: high oleic-acid sunflower oil)
  • handful of fresh parsley leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic (or more to taste)
  • extra-virgin olive oil (EVVO)
  • salt to taste (1-2 pinches)
  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180℃ fan-forced/convection (or 200℃ static)
  2. Prepare sardines:
    1. clean the sardines if they aren't already (you should at least remove gut - heads optional)
    2. rinse the sardines in cold water and tap dry
  3. Coat the fish in sunflower oil and salt
  4. Prepare garnishes
    1. chop fresh parsley into fine chunks
    2. mince garlic
    3. place EVOO small dish
      • combine all, give it a mix pan put aside
  5. When oven is preheated put the fish on a baking-sheet covered baking sheet and bake for approx. 12 min
    (or until fish are turning slightly golden and pale - meat should flake off with fork)
  6. Garnish the fish with olive oil mixed with garlic and parsley and bake for another minute or two
BONUS TIP: I love to have Greek-style salad along with sardines, or any type of fish marinaded with oil.
