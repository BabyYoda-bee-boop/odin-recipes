Greek-style salad

Description

A refreshing side dish to accompany a variety of main meat and fish dishes

Ingredients:

for

  • 1 fresh tomato
  • 1/4 cucumber
  • 1/4 red onion
  • 6-8 Kalamata olives (or other ones you love)
  • 40 g feta cheese
  • balsamic vinegar
  • extra-virgin olive oil (EVVO)
  • sea salt
  • optional: dried oregano
  1. Dice all the vegetables to prefered size
  2. Combine all ingredients, except feta cheese, and toss
  3. Add finely chopped or grated feta cheese and lightly toss again
